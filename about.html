Who We Are

Incorporated in 2022, TORR ENGINEERING has rapidly established itself as a premier authorized partner for global industrial leaders. We proudly represent Atlas Copco Vacuum Solutions, Idex Sandpiper, Idex Pulsafeeder, and Idex Cognito.

At TORR, our core competency lies in engineering robust infrastructure through high-performance industrial vacuum systems, modular piping architectures, and advanced fluid management solutions. Our commitment goes beyond mere supply; we provide expert technical consultation to identify, deploy, and maintain mission-critical equipment, ensuring operational excellence for clients across diverse sectors including manufacturing, automotive, electronics, and pharmaceuticals.
